The Approach

How the work unfolds

Every class and session at Inner Space follows the same quiet logic: settle the body, steady the breath, and turn attention inward — gently, and without forcing anything. From there, the practices begin to reveal what they're meant to.

Stillness

Before anything else, the body learns to settle. Most of the early work is simply this.

Awareness

Attention is trained like a muscle — first held, then refined, then allowed to rest on its own.

Energy

The subtler layers of experience — the energy body — are explored once the foundation is steady.

Integration

What's discovered on the cushion is brought back into daily life — that's where it matters most.
